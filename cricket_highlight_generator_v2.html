P1 — Core Events (base +30–60 pts)
Six (+40), Boundary (+30), Wicket (+38), Winning shot (+60), Hat-trick. Always included — they change the outcome.
P2 — Match Pressure (bonus up to +35 pts)
pressure field (1–10) × 3.5. A six in over 19 with 8 runs needed scores far higher than a six in over 2. Context is everything.
P3 — Player Milestones (+50–65 pts)
Half-century (+50), Century (+65), 5-wkt haul (+55). Always included. Set milestone column value to include these.
P4 — Crowd & Emotion (bonus up to +15 pts)
crowd field ≥15/20 adds (crowd-10)×1.5 bonus. Audio spikes, commentary excitement, player celebrations.
P5 — Shot Quality (+7–15 pts)
excellent (+15), good (+7). Cover drives, helicopter shots, ramp shots. Use in shot column.
P6 — Turning Points (+20 pts)
Set turning=true for momentum shifts, star-player wickets, collapses, or last-ball finishes.
